Roles we support

Delivery

Keep programmes moving with assets that are traceable to the original document without chasing handover folders, email threads, or re-opening finished work.

Popular use cases
  • Defects and variations verification
  • RFIs and as-built evidence
  • Handover that stays usable

Compliance

Stay audit ready with cited sources, clear change history, and controlled access, so warranty, safety, and regulatory questions do not turn into last minute scrambles.

Popular use cases
  • Audit trails and evidence packs
  • Safety and quality checks
  • Secure sharing for stakeholders

Operations

Run the asset on facts years after handover, with fast retrieval of what was installed, changed, tested, or approved, even when contractors and staff have moved on.

Popular use cases
  • Maintenance and shutdown decisions
  • Services and plant information retrieval
  • Faster incident investigation

Owners

Protect value during reviews and transactions with a reliable building record that reduces uncertainty, supports due diligence, and makes risk visible before it becomes cost.

Popular use cases
  • Portfolio documentation confidence
  • Pre sale and acquisition checks
  • Risk and liability visibility
